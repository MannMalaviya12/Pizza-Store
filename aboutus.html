About Us

Our Story

At Pizza Palace, we believe that great pizza starts with the finest ingredients and a passion for perfection. Our story began in 1985 when our founder, Tony, set out to create the best pizza in town. Since then, we've been serving up delicious pizzas that have become a favorite among our loyal customers.

We take pride in using only the freshest and locally-sourced ingredients for our pizzas. Our dough is made from scratch daily, and we carefully select the finest toppings to ensure every bite is a burst of flavor.

Our Mission

Our mission is simple: to provide our customers with the most delicious and satisfying pizza experience. We strive to exceed expectations by delivering exceptional service and the highest quality pizzas every time.

Our Team

Behind every great pizza is a team of dedicated and skilled individuals. Our team of pizza chefs, cooks, and delivery drivers work together to ensure that every pizza that leaves our kitchen is a masterpiece.

We believe in creating a positive and inclusive work environment where our team members can thrive and grow. Their passion for pizza and commitment to excellence is what sets us apart.
